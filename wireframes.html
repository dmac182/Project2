Key User-Flow for CRATE Crate - Xbox One App

Key User-Flow: Browse the crate sevices offered by CRATE Crate, look at specific product details, and put together a CRATE Crate subscription

Browse a list of all of the different crate services that CRATE Crate offers. The user picks different crate services to combine into one mega crate, which is then delivered to the user. The user can look at the crates that CRATE Crate offers, and see details for them and the type of content that they usually contain. The user must select which crates they want to be a part of their CRATE Crate, and then choose what sort of payment plan they want (1 month, 3 months, 6 months, etc.)
